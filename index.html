Angry Sheep

Interactive table experience for Children Museum of Pittsburgh

Angry Sheep is an interactive tabletop experience where children work together to herd sheep back to their pen using physical tools and verbal encouragement.

To accomplish the mission, children need to respond to the sheep's shifting emotions, calming them down when handling gets too rough.

The experience is designed to help young children build emotional resilience through collaborative play.

Lady Parts

Hybrid care system for stigma-free vaginal health on campus

Vaginal health is deeply personal and varies across individuals, yet widely stigmatized. For young individuals navigating reproductive health on their own for the first time, there's little guidance that fits their actual needs.

Lady Parts* is a university-based hybrid care system that pairs a self-screening tool and personalized questionnaire — "Vaginality Test" with a matched Care kit, turning an intimidating topic into an accessible, empowering experience.

Gold Winner

*Lady Parts — We are reclaiming the term “lady parts” to empower women and all vagina owners.

Manager Clone Agent

Full paper published at CHI 2026

AI systems are stepping into managerial roles, handling approvals, answering questions, and relaying decisions on behalf of busy leaders.

But when an agent is trained to impersonate specific managers, it borrows their voice, their credibility, and the trust that took years to build.

Through design fiction workshops with 23 workers and managers across industries, this paper surfaces the appeal and the risks of delegating human authority to a personalized AI clone.
